Hobby

My passion revolves around a tool that women use daily, makeup. Makeup, for me, has always brought me an immense amount of joy. When I began to wear makeup every day, I would spend time thinking about what makeup I would wear the next day and the next day. I would think about ways to improve myself. Sitting in front of my vanity, applying my makeup, I know it has never been about improving my face, but rather releasing creative energy through this type of art. This art has helped me to realize who I am meant to be. I want to help people realize that makeup is not about a disguise, but enhances the features that you already have.
