Last Year Summer Travels

Last year was full of travels.
First it started with going on short vacay to Greece with my grandparents. Then when i got back from Greece, went to Dojran for DFestival. It was awesome.

After that I booked a trip with my close friends. We went to Zakynthos and it went more than great. We met a lot of people there, had the most amazing time and gyro of course !

When I got back form Zante, I had about a day or two to repack my bags and go to Netherland. The weather there was nothing to compare with it here. It was cloudy and great for sleeping. Almost slept for about 15 hours. While I was in Netherlands, it wasn't a question not to visit Amsterdam, so when i Got there I saw the most breathtaking views and a lot of weed.
